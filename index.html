Which skill is the best to learn?: The consulting project with 80000hrs.org

Documentation, Codes

See the list of all skills along with descriptions by US Department of Labor here.

TAKE-HOME MESSAGE: keep challenging yourself by learning difficult skills. Such skills require connecting self, others and tasks. It will paid off: more income, more security, and slightly more satisfied.

Some insights

Insight #1: The best skills tend to be system and social skills. It requires the wide perspective on self, others, and tasks. These skills are difficult to master.

Insight #2: The worst skills tend to be technical skills. It requires the narrow perspectives on specific tools and tasks. Even though it is easy to master, it tends to associated with low paid and insecure jobs.
